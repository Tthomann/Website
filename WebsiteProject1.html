About Japan

Summary of the Land:

Made up of 6,852 islands that stretch across the coast of East Asia, Japan is an amazing place to go and explore. Although most of the land in Japan is unhabitable do to being too mountainous and forested, the countryside and city life make Japan a very interesting place to go. It also has 108 active volcanoes and is very prone to tsunamis caused by the earthquakes that Japan gets. There are many other interesting qualities that belong only to Japan.
