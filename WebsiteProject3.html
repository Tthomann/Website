About Japan

Art of Japan:

Art in Japan has been vastly mixed with European art. Woodblock printing and Western art styles birthed the creation of 'Manga' which is a form of comic book that reads right to left, like all literature in japan. On another note, when Manga became animated it was refered to as 'Anime' and is widely popular to this day across the country.
